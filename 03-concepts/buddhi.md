## Working definition

Buddhi (Sanskrit: बुद्धि) refers to the faculty of intelligence, discernment, and higher reasoning in Indian philosophical traditions. It is distinct from manas (lower mind) and represents a subtler faculty of knowing.

## Relevance to this project

Buddhi may represent an important non-Western lens for understanding what is meant by mind and intelligence in the E3 framework.
