# Project Scope

## Title

Human Mind Evolution

## Core inquiry

This project explores human evolution through a structured conceptual framework, currently centered on Equation 3 (E3).

The guiding question is:

**How did the evolutionary process produce not only a biologically advanced organism, but a being capable of mind, agency, self-modelling, symbolic culture, and world-shaping behavior?**

## Primary lens

The current working lens is Equation 3 (E3), understood as a model of the brain-culture feedback loop that may explain the emergence of human-level intelligence and agency.

## Objectives

- Develop a coherent framework for thinking about human evolution
- Clarify what Equation 3 means and what it explains
- Distinguish biological evolution from mind-level emergence
- Explore the role of culture, feedback, and symbolic systems
- Track open questions, alternative models, and unresolved tensions

## In scope

- Human evolution
- Brain evolution
- Culture and symbolic behavior
- Agency
- Self-modelling
- Intelligence and mind
- Conceptual models and philosophical implications

## Out of scope for now

- Detailed population genetics
- Technical neuroscience depth beyond current framework needs
- Exhaustive literature review before conceptual clarity
- Premature formalization of all equations before definitions stabilize

## Research style

This repository is exploratory but structured.

It is intended to function as:
- a research log,
- a conceptual workshop,
- a model-building space, and
- a long-horizon knowledge base.

## Current status

Equation 3 is the active starting framework.
