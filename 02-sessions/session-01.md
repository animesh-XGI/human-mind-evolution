# Session 01

## Date

2026-04-17

## Theme

Choosing the starting framework.

## Summary

This session established Equation 3 (E3) as the primary starting lens for the repository.

The central intuition was that E3 is the most powerful frame because it connects biology with mind, meaning, and agentic behavior through a reinforcing brain-culture loop.

## Key takeaways

- E3 was selected as the strongest initial framework
- The project will explore human emergence through the interaction of biology and culture
- The inquiry is not limited to physical evolution, but extends to mind, agency, and symbolic self-organisation
- A durable research log was identified as necessary
- GitHub was chosen as the main structured repository for this work

## Important framing

The working idea is that humans are not merely smarter animals in a linear sense.

Instead, the hypothesis is that a specific kind of recursive amplification occurred between evolving brains and evolving culture, eventually producing a new level of intelligence, agency, and self-modelling.

## Outputs from this session

- E3 chosen as active frame
- Research direction clarified
- Repo concept established
- Need for session logging and framework separation identified

## Open questions carried forward

- How should E3 be written more formally?
- Which variables belong inside the equation?
- Is the key phenomenon gradual or phase-transition-like?
- What is the relationship among mind, intelligence, and agency?
- Where does symbolic culture become causally decisive?
