## H1: Phase transition in human cognition

**Statement:** The emergence of human-level mind was not gradual in effect but represented a phase transition triggered by a threshold in the brain-culture feedback loop.

**Rationale:** The cultural and cognitive gap between humans and other primates appears disproportionate to the biological differences, suggesting a non-linear amplification effect.

**Status:** Proposed
