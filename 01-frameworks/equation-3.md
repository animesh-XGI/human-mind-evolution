# Equation 3 (E3)

## Status

Primary active framework.

## Working name

Equation 3 (E3)

## Provisional intuition

Equation 3 is the current attempt to explain human emergence not as a simple linear extension of animal intelligence, but as the result of a reinforcing loop between brain development and culture.

In this view, humans become possible when biological capacity and cultural transmission begin amplifying one another strongly enough to produce a phase change.

## Working statement

**Human-level mind emerges when brain capacity and culture enter a self-reinforcing loop, generating increasing symbolic depth, agency, self-modelling, and world-shaping power.**

## Core components

- Brain
- Culture
- Feedback loop
- Symbolic capacity
- Agency
- Self-modelling
- Cumulative transmission

## What E3 may explain

- Why human cognition appears discontinuous in effect, even if continuous in origin
- Why culture is not just an output of intelligence but part of the mechanism of human emergence
- Why human beings reshape environments, institutions, tools, and themselves
- Why agency becomes amplified through collective symbolic systems

## Open questions

- What exactly are the variables in E3?
- Is the loop gradual, thresholded, or phase-transition-like?
- How should mind be distinguished from intelligence in this framework?
- What role does self-modelling play?
- What are the strongest competing explanations?
